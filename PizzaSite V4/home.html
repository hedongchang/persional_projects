Welcome To Dawg Pizza!

Dawg Pizza is located at 4306 University Way NE, Seattle, WA 98105 in University of Washington and it has served campus commnity for almost 2 years. The goal of Dawg Pizza is to provide best dining services to all Huskies. Proudly, we made both traditional and non-traditional pizzas. If you are a real husky, don't forget to visit our place and order a pizza to show your Husky Pride! “ Go Huskies!!! ”

Awesome wood-fired pizza with fresh ingredients baked to order. Come in and eat, or call us for delivery!

Our business hours: 10:00am to 11:00pm, Monday through Saturday.

We proudly serve the community of University of Washington, the best university in Pacific Northwest. If you are not a Husky, welcome to explore UW campus!
